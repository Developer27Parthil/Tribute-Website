A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

A.P.J Abdul Kalam was Born in Rameswaram on October 15, 1931, graduating from Trichy and later specializing from Madras IIT in aero-engineering he became a formidable name in the development of missile technology, main battle tank, and light combat aircraft to ensure India defence capability against any foreign attack. Dr Kalam is a fitting reply to the common notion that without thorough western training no scientist can make any significant contribution to modern technology . He is a lover of music and poetry and a devotee of literature. Dr Kalam loves the young and believes that if properly educated they constitute India glorious future.
